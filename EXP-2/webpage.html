About Philips

Philips is a global leader in health technology, focused on improving people’s health and enabling better outcomes across the health continuum – from healthy living and prevention to diagnosis, treatment, and home care.

Our Mission

At Philips, our mission is to improve the lives of 3 billion people a year by 2030. We aim to make the world healthier and more sustainable through innovation and collaboration.

Our History

Philips was founded in Eindhoven, the Netherlands, in 1891 by Gerard Philips and his father Frederik. Originally, the company produced carbon-filament lamps and other electro-technical products. Over the years, Philips has evolved into a diversified health technology company, serving customers in more than 100 countries.

Our Values

  • Customer focus: We put our customers at the center of everything we do, striving to exceed their expectations and deliver innovative solutions that improve their lives.
  • Quality: We are committed to delivering products and services of the highest quality, adhering to the strictest standards and regulations.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and respect for all individuals, fostering trust and accountability.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation as the driving force behind our success, constantly seeking new ways to improve and create value for our customers and stakeholders.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to making a positive impact on the world by promoting sustainability in everything we do, from our products and operations to our partnerships and community engagement.
